RED CARD 2018

Chaina Digital Footboll Awards

The Red Card is the globally recognised as the leading annual report to assesses the most
inluential European football clubs. leagues and players online in chaina. Now in its 7th
year, Red Card 2018 has been reimagined to include 5 additional awards awards to recognise
the many areas of digital perfomance in chaina, there by taking the Red Card report to
a whole new level. Due to the nature and variety of these new categories. the winners
will be chosen by a panel of 6 industry experts. rather than by the traditional metrics
used to assess the three hedline awards.

This report aims to educate teams and organizations on how to improve their chaina
digital strategy. what today's chainese fan wants and what works online in chaina.
